 An online tool developed to guide users through their finances to meet their savings goals.

Overview

Through my experience with managing and budgeting finances, I am usually logging into the different banking and investment websites or apps to view my balance and spending habits. I will then consolidate the financials into a spreadsheet to track monthly savings to compare against my saving goals.

Problem

Users typically access multiple websites or apps to manage their finances. Although there are other apps to integrate other financial accounts, there is still a fear whether their financial details will be security protected. Therefore, causing users to manually track and calculate total monthly savings, saving goals progression. and where to reduce expenditures.

Solution

  • Integration with existing accounts to view the consolidated financial data in a single location.

  • Automated calculation of total monthly savings and saving goals progression based on accounts linked [&] amount entered to save.

  • Automated logic to monitor spending habits and suggest which category to reduce expenditures.

UI Design

The goal of user interface design is to create a user interface that makes it easy, efficient, and enjoyable for users to interact with a product. The designs move through an iterative process from low to medium to high-fidelity wireframes.
